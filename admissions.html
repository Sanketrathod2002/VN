Step-by-Step Admission Process

A simple, transparent, and merit-based procedure to join our educational complex.

1. Online Enquiry

Fill out the detailed admission enquiry form below to register your interest in our programs.

2. Document Submission

Visit the campus office to submit your application form along with verified academic and identity documents.

3. Merit & Verification

For higher secondary and degree courses, wait for the merit list. Documents will be strictly verified.

4. Fee Confirmation

Pay the admission fees or submit scholarship/freeship forms to formally secure your seat.
