Campus & Facilities

Home / Facilities

At Jai Janta Education Society, we believe that a conducive environment is just as important as classroom learning. Our Kinhiraja campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, safe residential facilities, and specialized laboratories to ensure our students have everything they need to succeed academically and personally.

Residential Ashram School & Hostels

Our Ashram schools provide a safe, secure, and nurturing home away from home for students from rural and marginalized backgrounds. We focus on holistic development, ensuring students receive proper nutrition, daily routine discipline, and an environment conducive to continuous learning.

  • Separate, well-ventilated dormitories for boys and girls.
  • Hygienic mess providing nutritious, balanced daily meals.
  • 24/7 campus security and resident wardens.
  • Emergency medical assistance and regular health checkups.

Academic & Campus Amenities

Infrastructure designed to support modern education and physical well-being.

Advanced Science Labs

Fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories for our Higher Secondary Science students, ensuring hands-on practical learning in alignment with board guidelines.

Computer & IT Center

Modern computer labs with high-speed internet access. Crucial for our MCVC vocational students and for teaching essential digital literacy across all departments.

Central Library

A vast repository of academic books, journals, and reference materials. Features a dedicated quiet reading room and resources for students preparing for competitive exams.

Sports & Playground

A sprawling playground featuring courts for Volleyball, grounds for Cricket, and facilities for athletics to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and competitive sportsmanship.

Smart Classrooms

Select classrooms equipped with digital smart boards and audio-visual aids to make complex subjects easier to understand through interactive learning.

Vocational Workshops

Specialized workshops equipped with modern tools and machinery for MCVC students studying Electrical Technology, Auto Engineering, and Electronics.

About Society

Jai Janta Education Society, Fulumari Ta. Manora Dist. Washim is dedicated to providing quality education, skill development, and strong moral values to rural students in Kinhiraja, Washim.
