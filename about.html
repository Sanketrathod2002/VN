Our Journey & Legacy

Jai Janta Education Society, Fulumari Ta. Manora Dist. Washim, operating through our expansive educational complex in Kinhiraja, is a beacon of knowledge and empowerment for rural youth. Founded with a deep commitment to social upliftment, our society has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution providing education from the primary level all the way to senior degree courses.

Our campus houses six distinct institutions, ranging from Ashram Schools that provide residential education to marginalized communities, to specialized Science, Arts, MCVC, and Senior Degree Colleges. We believe that true education goes beyond textbooks; it is about building character, instilling values, and equipping students with the modern skills necessary to thrive in a competitive world.

Driven by the ideology of our great inspiration, Late Vishal Bhau Chavhan, we continually strive to bridge the educational divide, ensuring that every student in the Washim district has access to high-quality, holistic, and technology-driven education.

Our Philosophy

The guiding principles that shape our educational approach.

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier educational hub in rural Maharashtra that transforms lives through quality education, empowering students to become socially responsible, culturally rooted, and globally competent citizens.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and holistic education. We aim to foster academic excellence, vocational skills, and moral integrity, ensuring every student reaches their highest potential in a secure and nurturing environment.

Core Values

We are guided by the principles of Knowledge, Character, and Service. We believe in inclusive growth, discipline, innovation in teaching, and profound respect for our rich cultural heritage.

From the Principal's Desk

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Welcome to the Jai Janta Education Society campus in Kinhiraja. It gives me immense pleasure to lead an institution that is deeply committed to the intellectual, physical, and moral development of our students. Our focus has always been on providing a rigorous academic framework coupled with rich extracurricular opportunities.

In today's rapidly changing world, it is essential that we prepare our youth not just with degrees, but with real-world skills, critical thinking, and a strong ethical compass. Our dedicated faculty members work tirelessly to ensure that every student, regardless of their background, receives personalized attention and the best possible resources.

Through our various departments—from Primary Ashram Schools to our Senior Degree College—we are building a seamless pathway for lifelong learning. I invite parents and students to join hands with us in this noble journey of education and empowerment.
