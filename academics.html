Academic Programs

Home / Academics

Jai Janta Education Society provides a continuous and comprehensive educational pathway from standard 1st up to graduate degrees. Our curriculum is designed strictly as per the State Board and affiliated University guidelines, ensuring students receive recognized, high-quality instruction augmented with practical skills and ethical grounding.

Baliram Appa Gondal Primary Ashram School

Building a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our primary section focuses on developing basic literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a nurturing environment tailored for young minds.

1st to 4th Standard Marathi Medium

Core Subjects

  • Marathi (First Language)
  • English
  • Hindi
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • History
  • Geography

Vasantrao Naik Secondary Ashram School

Preparing students for the crucial SSC Board Examinations. We offer both Marathi and Semi-English mediums to cater to diverse student backgrounds while maintaining high academic rigor.

5th to 10th Standard Marathi Medium

Curriculum Overview

  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi, English
  • Mathematics (Algebra & Geometry)
  • General Science
  • Social Sciences (History, Civics, Geography)
  • Information Technology & Personality Development
5th to 10th Standard Semi-English Medium

Curriculum Overview

  • Languages: Marathi, Hindi, English
  • Mathematics (Taught in English)
  • General Science (Taught in English)
  • Social Sciences (Taught in Marathi)
  • Information Technology

Our Teaching Methodology

Across all our institutions, we employ a student-centric approach. We blend traditional classroom teaching with modern digital aids, practical laboratory sessions, field visits, and continuous comprehensive evaluation (CCE). We believe in inclusive education where remedial coaching is provided for slow learners, and advanced guidance is available for competitive examinations.
